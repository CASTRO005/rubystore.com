ABOUT US


Welcome to Ruby Store – your ultimate destination for cutting-edge technology and personalized service. Founded by Adithya Nethsarani, an entrepreneurial visionary with a passion for innovation, Ruby Store is a leading computer shop committed to meeting the diverse needs of our community.

At Ruby Store, we offer a carefully curated selection of top-quality computer hardware, software, and accessories. Whether you're a gamer, creative professional, or a business seeking reliable IT solutions, our store is designed to cater to your unique requirements.

Our team of knowledgeable and friendly experts is dedicated to providing exceptional customer service. We understand that technology is personal, and we're here to help you find the perfect solutions tailored to your specific needs.

In addition to our extensive product range, Ruby Store provides expert technical support and repair services. Our certified technicians are ready to assist with everything from hardware upgrades to software troubleshooting, ensuring your devices run smoothly.

As a locally owned and operated business, Ruby Store takes pride in being an integral part of the community. Beyond being a retail space, our store is a dynamic hub where tech enthusiasts can connect, learn, and stay informed about the latest trends in the ever-evolving world of technology. Experience the Ruby Store difference – where technology meets personalized service. Visit us today and embark on a journey of tech excellence!
